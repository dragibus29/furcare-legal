Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2024

1. Introduction and Legal Basis

FurCare ("we," "our," or "us") processes personal data in accordance with:

  • The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • The UK Data Protection Act 2018
  • The Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR)

2. Data Controller

For the purposes of applicable data protection laws, FurCare is the data controller of your personal information. Contact: support@furcare.anonaddy.com

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Essential Data

  • Device camera access (with explicit permission)
  • Photo library access (with explicit permission)
  • Basic pet profile information (name, breed)
  • Health records and analysis history (stored locally on device)
  • Pet health entries (medications, vaccinations, weight, grooming)
  • Stool analysis results and images
  • Veterinary and grooming appointment records

3.2 Technical Data

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version information
  • Crash reports and performance data

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR) - To provide our service
  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) - To improve our service
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR) - For camera and photo access

5. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide stool analysis results through OpenAI's API
  • To maintain your pet's health history (stored locally)
  • To track pet health records and appointments
  • To improve our analysis accuracy
  • To comply with legal obligations

6. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device only
  • No personal or pet data is transmitted or stored on external servers
  • Images are processed securely and immediately discarded
  • Analysis results remain on your device only
  • Health records are stored locally and can be deleted at any time

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

  • Right to access (Article 15)
  • Right to rectification (Article 16)
  • Right to erasure (Article 17)
  • Right to restrict processing (Article 18)
  • Right to data portability (Article 20)
  • Right to object (Article 21)

8. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services. You can delete your data at any time through the app settings.

If you notice any of these symptoms, seek immediate veterinary care:

  • Blood in stool
  • Severe diarrhea or constipation
  • Lethargy or weakness
  • Loss of appetite
  • Vomiting
  • Unusual behavior
  • Changes in stool consistency or color
  • Difficulty defecating
  • Signs of pain or discomfort
